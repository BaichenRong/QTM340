Rose Manuscript, Archives and Rare Book Library acquired a new collection of papersfrom poet Richard Blanco, who delivered his poem "One Today" at former U.S. President BarackObama's inauguration in 2013, according to a Feb. 21 University press release. The papers include firsteditions of Blanco's books, commissioned and occasional poems, correspondence with other writersand unpublished writing.
