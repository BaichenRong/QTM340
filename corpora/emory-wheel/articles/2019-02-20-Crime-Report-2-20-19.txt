On Feb. 8 at 11:16 p.m., Emory Police Department (EPD) responded to a fire alarm at ClairmontResidential Center (CRC). DeKalb County Fire Rescue and Emory Facilities Management were alsodispatched to the scene. One officer noticed that a sprinkler head in the hallway of the third floor wasbroken off and that water was pouring out of a pipe where the sprinkler head was supposed to be. Theofficer notified Facilities Management personnel, who believed someone hit the sprinkler head, triggeringthe fire alarm. Facilities Management successfully turned the water off and silenced the fire alarm butwere unable to reset the fire alarm system. Facilities Management stated they would conduct a firewatch until the issue was resolved. EPD notified the fire safety coordinator and fire safety director as wellas Campus Life about the incident.
