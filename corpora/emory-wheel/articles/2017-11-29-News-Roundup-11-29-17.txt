University Senate Votes to Send Tax Act Protest LetterThe Emory University Senate voted Tuesday to send letters to U.S. representatives and senators forGeorgia to express concerns for the proposed Tax Cuts and Jobs Act. In particular, the letters mentionthat the House bill repeals U.S. Code sections 117(d), which allows individuals to deduct qualified tuitionexpenses from gross income, and section 127, which excludes employer assistance with tuitionpayments from gross income. "Taxing the scholarships and educational assistance described in thesesections would put higher education out of reach for many of our employees and their families," theletters read.
